About Me

I Dr.Vikram Kumar, working as scientist(Hydrology) at Bihar Mausam Seva Kendra, Department of Planning and Development, Government of Bihar, Patna.I have experience in water resource management and field hydrological instrumentation.Also I am good at hydrological data assimilation and monitoring. Recently I have started working on climatology of Bihar state of India and urban storm water management for different applications.
